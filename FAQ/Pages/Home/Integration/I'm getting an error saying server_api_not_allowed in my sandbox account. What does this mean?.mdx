---
title: I'm getting an error saying server_api_not_allowed in my sandbox account. What does this mean?
account: FAQ 
---
This error occurs when the full API isn't enabled on your sandbox account. To enable it, contact our Integration team at [integration@checkout.com](mailto:integration@checkout.com).
