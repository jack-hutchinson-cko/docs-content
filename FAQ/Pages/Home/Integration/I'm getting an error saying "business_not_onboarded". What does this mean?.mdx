---
title: I'm getting an error saying "business_not_onboarded". What does this mean?
account: FAQ 
---

This error occurs when an alternative payment method has been configured for your account but the specific credentials you need to create payments for that method have not been set up.

Please contact your customer success manager or implementation engineer to resolve this.
