For the **test (sandbox) environment**, please email [integration@checkout.com](mailto:integration@checkout.com) to add new payment methods to your account.

For the **live environment**, please get in touch with your customer success manager to add new payment methods to your account.

You can find out more about the payment methods we support on our [documentation site](/display/DOCS/Payment+methods).
