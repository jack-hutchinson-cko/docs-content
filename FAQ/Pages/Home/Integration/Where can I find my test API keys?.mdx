---
title: Where can I find my test API keys?
account: FAQ 
---
You can find your test keys in your sandbox Hub account under **Settings > Channels**. Test keys use the prefix `sk_test` and `pk_test`.

Always use your test keys when testing your integration; this is how we know to link all test payments to your sandbox account.
