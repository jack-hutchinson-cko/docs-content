title: How do I set a dynamic billing descriptor?
account: FAQ 
---

In order to enable dynamic billing descriptors on your account, please contact your customer success manager.

Once they are enabled, simply follow the instructions in [this guide](/display/DOCS/Dynamic+billing+descriptor). Note that you can currently only set billing descriptors for payment requests.
