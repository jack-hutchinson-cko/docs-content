---
title: How do I check the status of a payment?
account: FAQ 
---

If you want to check the status of a payment on the Hub, log in to your account and then go to the _Payments_ screen, where you'll find the full payment activity for your business. Read our guide on [managing payments from the Hub](/display/DOCS/Manage+Hub+payments) for more details.

Alternatively, use the [get payment details endpoint](/display/DOCS/Get+payment+details) to check the status of a specific payment.
