To tally up your statements and reports, you can [use our `/statements` endpoint’s payment extension](https://docs.checkout.com/reporting-and-insights/reconciliation-api/statements-endpoint#Statementsendpoint-2.StatementID/PaymentsstatementIDpayments). It allows you to retrieve the list of payments and associated fees that are included in a statement or payout which has been deposited into your bank account, or a specific payout currency.
