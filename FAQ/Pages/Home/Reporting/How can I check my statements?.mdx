The _Statements_ screen in the [Hub](https://hub.checkout.com) allows you to view your current statement and download all previous ones.

Alternatively, you can use the [`/statements` endpoint](/display/DOCS/Statements+endpoint) of the Reconciliation API to call a summary of your statements and payouts within specified parameters.
