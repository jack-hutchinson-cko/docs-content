You can log in to the Hub by going to [hub.checkout.com](https://hub.checkout.com). Use the account credentials provided to you in your account-creation email.

To log in to your sandbox environment (using the same credentials as your live account), [click here](https://sandbox.checkout.com/login).

If you don't have an account yet, [sign up for a test account](https://www.checkout.com/get-test-account).
