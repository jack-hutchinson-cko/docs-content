---
title: What will happen to my funds after I terminate my account?
account: FAQ 
---
All funds (the settlement payouts and cumulative reserves) will be kept by [Checkout.com](http://Checkout.com) until all reserves are released. The exact time the funds will be held is set out in your Merchant Services Agreement; it will be a number of weeks after the last transaction date.
