Learn how to [add](doc:broadcast-webhooks-the-hub#section-adding-a-webhook), [edit](doc:broadcast-webhooks-the-hub#section-editing-a-webhook), and [delete](doc:broadcast-webhooks-the-hub#section-deleting-a-webhook) your Broadcast webhook endpoints from inside The Hub. ## Locating Broadcast endpoint management Follow these steps to find the Broadcast control panel in The Hub. <span class="step\_hl">Step 1:</span> Log in to _The Hub_. <span class="step\_hl">Step 2:</span> Click the :fa-cog: (cog) icon in the top right. <span class="step\_hl">Step 3:</span> Click to select your business name in the left column. <span class="step\_hl">Step 4:</span> Select _Channel Settings_ from the top navigation. <span class="step\_hl">Step 5:</span> Click _View_ on the right of your website API info.

After following these instructions, you can view the Broadcast webhook endpoint control panel. Here you can Add, View, Edit, and Delete your webhooks.

Before starting, make sure you follow the instructions in [Locating Broadcast webhook management](doc:broadcast-webhooks-the-hub#section-locating-broadcast-endpoint-management). From here you can add a webhook. <span class="step\_hl">Step 1:</span> Click Add, in the webhook management panel. <span class="step\_hl">Step 2:</span> Click Edit to configure this webhook.

Before starting, make sure you follow the instructions in [Locating Broadcast webhook management](doc:broadcast-webhooks-1#section-locating-broadcast-webhook-management), and have previously [added a webhook](doc:broadcast-webhooks-1#section-adding-a-webhook). From here you can edit a webhook. <span class="step\_hl">Step 1:</span> Click _Edit_ to the right of the endpoint you want to change. <span class="step\_hl">Step 2:</span> Change the endpoint URL or the event type (or both). <span class="step\_hl">Step 3:</span> Click _Ok_ to return to the webhook management screen. <span class="step\_hl">Step 4:</span> Click _Save and Close_ to save your edited endpoint.

Before starting, make sure you follow the instructions in [Locating Broadcast webhook management](doc:broadcast-webhooks-the-hub#section-locating-broadcast-endpoint-management), and have previously [added a webhook](doc:broadcast-webhooks-the-hub#section-adding-a-webhook). From here you can delete a webhook. <span class="step\_hl">Step 1:</span> Click _Delete_ at the bottom left. <span class="step\_hl">Step 2:</span> If you're sure, click _Confirm_. <span class="step\_hl">Step 3:</span> Click _Save and Close_ to save the action.
