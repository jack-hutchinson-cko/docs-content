Before starting, make sure you follow the instructions in \[Locating Broadcast webhook management\](doc:broadcast-webhooks-1#section-locating-broadcast-webhook-management), and have previously \[added a webhook\](doc:broadcast-webhooks-1#section-adding-a-webhook). From here you can edit a webhook. <span class="step\_hl">Step 1:</span> Click \*Edit\* to the right of the endpoint you want to change. <span class="step\_hl">Step 2:</span> Change the endpoint URL or the event type (or both). <span class="step\_hl">Step 3:</span> Click \*Ok\* to return to the webhook management screen. <span class="step\_hl">Step 4:</span> Click \*Save and Close\* to save your edited endpoint. 

Before starting, make sure you follow the instructions in \[Locating Broadcast webhook management\](doc:broadcast-webhooks-the-hub#section-locating-broadcast-endpoint-management), and have previously \[added a webhook\](doc:broadcast-webhooks-the-hub#section-adding-a-webhook). From here you can delete a webhook. <span class="step\_hl">Step 1:</span> Click \*Delete\* at the bottom left. <span class="step\_hl">Step 2:</span> If you're sure, click \*Confirm\*. <span class="step\_hl">Step 3:</span> Click \*Save and Close\* to save the action.
