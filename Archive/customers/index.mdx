\## Search Use the search bar to identify customers based on a specific word, string of words or value. # Filter Use the filter feature to display customers who meet specific criteria. Click the \*filter\* icon to establish a filter. Note that number to the left of each filter row represents the number of customers matching your filter criteria. 

Select any \*Customer ID \*to open the Customer Details screen, which displays detailed information about the customer and the customer's transaction history. The buttons on the bottom left corner allow you to \*Update Customer Details\* (name, email, phone number), \*Add a Credit Card\* and \*Create a Payment\* (see below). The screen also lists any cards and local payment methods associated with the customer, which can be edited.

\## Creating a payment Click the \*Create Payment\* button at the bottom of the Customer Details screen to create a payment using one of the customer's saved cards. Select a saved card from the drop-down menu and fill in the remaining required fields, marked in red. Click More Options to enter additional details such as Billing Address, Shipping Address and customer phone number. To create the payment, press the \*Save\* button.
