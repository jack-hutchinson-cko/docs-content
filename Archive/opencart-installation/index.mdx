\*Install our Checkout.com extension on your OpenCart site and start accepting payments now.\* 

1\. Download the extension from \[GitHub\](https://github.com/CKOTech/checkout-opencart-plugin). 2. Upload the contents of the extension to... \`\[Root Directory\]/upload/admin/...\` \`\[Root Directory\]/upload/catalog/...\` 3. Login to your OpenCart admin panel. 4. Navigate to \`Extension > Payments\`. 5. Find \*\*Checkout.com(Gateway 3.0)\*\* in the Payment list. 6. Click the \*\*\[+\]\*\* icon to install the Checkout.com extension 

Configure your extension by following these simple steps. 1. Click on the \*\*\[Pen\]\*\* icon to configure the payment module. 2. Enter your \*\*Secret Key\*\* and \*\*Public Key\*\* - \*You can find this in \[The Hub\](https://hub.checkout.com/)\* 3. \*\*Is your website PCI Compliant?\*\* i. \*\*Yes\*\* - Merchant's website should be PCI compliant. ii. \*\*No\*\* - Use Checkout.js 4. \*\*Select Payment Action Type:\*\* Decide which option is best for your business. i. \*\*Authorize Only \*\* Transactions will only authorize and must be captured manually. ii. \*\*Authorize and Capture\*\* Transactions are authorized and captured automatically. \*With authorize and capture, an auto-capture time can be set. Auto-capture time defines how long after an authorization that a capture will be made â€” the default time is 0.\*
