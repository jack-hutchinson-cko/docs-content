The Actions dropdown menu allows you to **capture**, **void**, **blacklist** or **refund** multiple transactions. Tick the checkbox to the left of your chosen transactions or tick the top left checkbox to select all transactions. Choose an action from the Action dropdown menu. The menu will automatically display a list of available actions shared between the selected transactions. If no actions are available, the menu will be disabled.

The **create payment** button is disabled by default. Please contact your relationship manager if you require this functionality.

If you receive mail orders or telephone orders (MOTO payments), you can easily accept these payments by clicking the **Create Payment ** button located above the Transactions Table. To create a payment, fill out the required fields (marked in red) and click **Save**.

The capture amount cannot exceed the authorised amount. Partial captures are allowed, but the total capture amount cannot exceed the authorised amount.

To capture an authorized transaction, tick the checkbox next to the transaction and choose _Capture_ from the _action_ dropdown menu. Select _Full Capture_ or _Partial Capture_, enter a note (optional) and click _Capture_ to complete the transaction.

To void an authorised transaction, tick the checkbox next to the transaction and choose Void from the Action dropdown menu. Enter a note (optional) and click Void to complete the transaction.

A transaction can be refunded within 120 days of its original transaction date. Partial and multiple refunds are allowed, but the total refund amount cannot exceed the captured amount.

To issue a refund on a captured transaction, tick the checkbox next to the transaction and choose Refund from the _Action_ dropdown menu. Select _Full Refund_ or _Partial Refund_, enter a note (optional) and click _Refund_ to complete the transactions.

### Example A transaction is authorised for $50.00 USD. It is then captured for $30.00 USD. This captured transaction can be refunded for up to $30.00 USD. You can issue multiple refunds totalling $30.00 (e.g. Refund 1: $20.00 USD, Refund 2: $30.00 USD) or a single refund of $30.00 USD.

To blacklist an attribute or attributes, tick the checkbox next to the transaction and choose _Blacklist_ from the _Action_ dropdown menu. Select the attribute(s) you wish to blacklist and click _Blacklist_. In the future, any transaction matching a blacklisted attribute will be declined.
