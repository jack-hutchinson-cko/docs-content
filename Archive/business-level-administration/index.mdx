Select one of your businesses (listed under your account name) to access the Payout Settings and Channel Settings for the selected business. 

The Payout Settings screen is read-only and lists the details of the payout account(s) for the business you've selected.

Click \*Manage\* to configure your webhook URL. Enter your desired webhook URL and click \*Save\*. This will automatically generate a private shared key you can use to authenticate webhook messages sent by Checkout.com to your specified URL. You can edit your webhook URL at any time by clicking \*Edit\*, followed by \*Save\*. To refresh your private shared key, click \*Refresh\* and enter your login password for The Hub. Click \*Submit\* to confirm the refresh. \* Click Test to send a test webhook to your specified address at any time. \* Click Delete to delete the webhook URL from your System Admin settings. \* Click OK to close the Webhook Management window.
