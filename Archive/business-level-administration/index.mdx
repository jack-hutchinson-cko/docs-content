Select one of your businesses (listed under your account name) to access the Payout Settings and Channel Settings for the selected business.

The Payout Settings screen is read-only and lists the details of the payout account(s) for the business you've selected.

---

![](https://files.readme.io/1adbcaa-merchant-admin-payout-settings.png)

![](https://files.readme.io/1adbcaa-merchant-admin-payout-settings.png)**merchant-admin-payout-settings.png**

In addition to bank account information, the Payout Settings screen also includes the following:

To update any of the details shown in the Payout Settings screen, please contact your Relationship Manager.

The Channel Settings screen allows you to configure certain settings for the business channel(s) linked to the business you've selected.

Click the blue arrow to expand your view and reveal your API Keys.

Click _View_ to manage your URLs, Webhooks and API Keys.

![](https://files.readme.io/551f53a-merchant-admin-manage-urls.png)

![](https://files.readme.io/551f53a-merchant-admin-manage-urls.png)**merchant-admin-manage-urls.png**

Click _Edit_ to edit your Redirection and Redirection Fail URLs. To save your changes, click _Save_.

---

![](https://files.readme.io/269f8de-merchant-admin-update-urls.png)

![](https://files.readme.io/269f8de-merchant-admin-update-urls.png)**merchant-admin-update-urls.png**

Click _Manage_ to configure your webhook URL. Enter your desired webhook URL and click _Save_. This will automatically generate a private shared key you can use to authenticate webhook messages sent by Checkout.com to your specified URL. You can edit your webhook URL at any time by clicking _Edit_, followed by _Save_. To refresh your private shared key, click _Refresh_ and enter your login password for The Hub. Click _Submit_ to confirm the refresh. _ Click Test to send a test webhook to your specified address at any time. _ Click Delete to delete the webhook URL from your System Admin settings. \* Click OK to close the Webhook Management window.
