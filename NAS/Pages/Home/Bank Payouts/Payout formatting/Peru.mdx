Account number

The account holder's account number (Código de Cuenta Interbancario, or CCI).

`account_number`

REQUIRED

STRING, ^[0-9]{20,20}$

SWIFT BIC

The account holder's SWIFT BIC.

`swift_bic`

REQUIRED

STRING, ^[A-Z]{6}[A-Z0-9]{2}([A-Z0-9]{3})?$
